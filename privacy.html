privacy policy

Lichen Protocol is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information submitted through our forms.

what we collect

When you submit a form, we collect:

  • Your name and email address
  • Your responses to questionnaire and check-in questions
  • Any optional information you choose to provide

how we use your information

We use your information to:

  • Understand your current field conditions and support your journey
  • Track shifts and integration over time
  • Improve the Lichen Protocol system
  • Contact you about your sessions when necessary

data storage and security

Your submissions are securely stored using Netlify Forms. We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

sharing your information

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except as required by law or with your explicit consent.

your rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your data
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, please contact us at nigel@lichenprotocol.com.
